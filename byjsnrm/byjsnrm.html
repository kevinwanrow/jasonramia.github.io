What is a Mini-Book?  
What are mini-books? They’re FREE downloadable PDF ebooks that I have written. They can be poetry, short stories, dialogues, etc... There is no minimum or maximum limit of pages, no specific structure, but simply a way for me to put my writings in one place where the reader can find them. These mini-books exist solely to entertain readers and for them to delve into, whether they’re on a break, waiting for something or someone, or simply like to read.


Please do not redistribute any of the mini-books, they are given to you & you only.


The mini-books are not intended for children unless explicitly stated otherwise.


- Jason Ramia
